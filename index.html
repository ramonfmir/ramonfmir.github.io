I am a first year Computer Science PhD student at the University of Edinburgh. I work at the Laboratory for Foundations of Computer Science (LFCS) under the supervision of Dr. Paul Jackson. Previously, I studied at Imperial College London and the Institute for Logic, Language and Computation (ILLC).

My research interests lie primarily in the area of formal methods, including:

  • Formal verification of cyber-physical systems.
  • Safe and explainable AI.
  • Interactive and automated theorem proving.
  • Functional programming languages.
  • Formalised mathematics.
