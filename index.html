Ramon Fernández Mir

I am a first year Computer Science PhD student at the University of Edinburgh. I work at the Laboratory for Foundations of Computer Science (LFCS) under the supervision of Dr. Paul Jackson. Previously, I studied Maths and Computer Science at Imperial College London and Logic at the Institute for Logic, Language and Computation (ILLC).

My research interests concern the development of formal methods to ensure the correctness and reliability of certain systems. More specifically, I am investigating the following topics:

  • Formal verification of cyber-physical systems (e.g. autonomous vehicles).
  • Safe and explainable AI.
  • Interactive and automated theorem proving.
  • Functional programming languages.
  • Formalised mathematics.
